keyword: sum
    pop the last 2 numbers and push the result of their addition

keyword: mul
    pop the last 2 numbers and push the result of their multiply

keyword: sub
    pop the last 2 numbers and push the result of their subtraction

keyword: div
    pop the last 2 numbers and push the result of their division

keyword: display
    print the top element of the stack on the terminal


writing any integer in an newline adds it to the stack
